About me

Robotics engineer who's about AI products.

When internet became a consumer commodity, many shared concerns about their jobs becoming redundant as a result. The same have been told for all revolutionary technologies and tools. And everytime, they have generated more jobs than they ended. Anecdotes like has been said a zillion times already. It's a bit cliche.at this point.

I stand on the optimistic side.

My ML / software journey began with my passion for aviation, which led me to build RC planes in high school as hobby, and then pursue studies to be able to build autonomous aerial vehicles. In this pursuit, I studied computer vision, deep learning, autonomous navigation, fluid dynamics, robot perception, robot localization and then.... NVIDIA and transformers happened, driving my curiosity further in deep learning based perception systems.
With all my learnings, which are forever on-going of course, I am enjoying my full stack AI product journey where I build, test, sell and ship AI products. It is thrilling to explore what AI and readily available cloud GPU compute is empowering us / me to build. Either to enhance user experience or eliminate repetitive tasks. ClosetX and Sightly, being the latest personal endeavours outside of my work life, wherein I try to leverage AI to build tools that enhance consumer experience in two different industries.

If you build autonomous vehicles or software that leverages AI-GenAI to enhance user experience in fashion, real estate decor, insurance, strategy or any other exciting space, feel free to reach out to me through my email for a coffee chat.
