About me

Robotics engineer who's about AI products.
When the internet became a consumer commodity, many shared concerns about their jobs becoming redundant as a result. The same has been said for all revolutionary technologies and tools. And every time, they have generated more jobs than they ended. Anecdotes like these have been said a zillion times already. It's a bit cliche at this point.

I stand on the optimistic side.

My ML/software journey began with my passion for aviation, which led me to build RC planes in high school as a hobby and then pursue studies to be able to build autonomous aerial vehicles. In this pursuit, I studied computer vision, deep learning, autonomous navigation, fluid dynamics, robot perception, robot localisation, and then... NVIDIA and transformers happened, driving my curiosity further in deep learning-based perception systems.
With all my learnings, which are forever on-going, of course, I am enjoying my full-stack AI product journey where I build, test, sell, and ship AI products. It is thrilling to explore what AI and readily available cloud GPU compute is empowering us/me to build. ClosetX and Timelight are the latest personal endeavours outside of my work life, wherein I try to leverage AI to build tools that enhance consumer experience in two different industries.
If you build autonomous vehicles or software that leverages AI-GenAI to enhance the user experience in fashion, real estate decor, insurance, strategy, or any other exciting space, feel free to reach out to me through my email for a coffee chat.
